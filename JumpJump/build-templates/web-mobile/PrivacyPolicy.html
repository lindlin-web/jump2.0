2. How We Use Cookies and Related Technologies

Cookies are a neutral technology that allows web servers to store data on or retrieve data from the client. To optimize your experience, we use cookies or similar technologies (such as web beacons, log files, scripts, eTags) (collectively referred to as "Cookies"). Cookies will generate small files on your device to provide specific features. You have the right to enable or disable cookies through your device or browser settings. However, disabling cookies may prevent you from experiencing some features of our products.

We may use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain services: Ensure our services function properly and provide necessary support.
  • To improve and optimize services: Improve our products and services by analyzing user behavior and feedback.
  • To personalize user experience: Provide customized content and recommendations to enhance your gaming experience.
  • To process transactions and send related information: Process your payments, provide purchase confirmations, and related customer service.
  • To communicate: Send you updates about the game, promotions, events, and other game-related messages.
  • To comply with legal requirements: Ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations, protect our rights and property.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security.

6. How We Store and Protect Your Personal Information

6.1 Storage of Personal Information

6.1.1 Method and Duration of Storage

We securely store your information using methods such as local storage (e.g., data caching via the app), databases, and server logs. We will only store your personal information for the time necessary to achieve the service purpose or as required by laws and regulations. Unless otherwise specified by law or you request deletion under this privacy policy, we will retain your personal information during the operation of the game.

6.1.2 Deletion of Information

When the game ceases operation or when we no longer retain your information according to this privacy policy, the electronic files storing personal information will be permanently deleted. We will take reasonable measures to ensure personal information cannot be recovered. If the law requires us to continue retaining your personal information, it will be stored separately from other types of information.

6.2 Protection of Personal Information

6.2.1 Information Security

We take technical and management measures to ensure the security of your account and personal information. We use industry-leading security measures such as firewalls, encryption (e.g., SSL), de-identification or anonymization, access control (bastion hosts, two-factor authentication), and more. We have established special management systems, processes, and organizations to protect personal information security. Only employees who need to know this information can access your personal information, and we have strict access control and monitoring mechanisms. We also require all personnel who may access your information to fulfill confidentiality obligations. If they fail to meet these obligations, we will pursue legal responsibility.

6.2.2 Handling Information Security Incidents

In the event of a personal information leak or other security incident, we will activate an emergency plan to prevent the incident from expanding. We will inform you of the incident through announcements, push notifications, or emails, explaining the basic situation, the measures we have taken or will take, and providing you with suggestions. If it is difficult to notify each individual, we will release warnings through announcements. We will do our best to protect your personal information, but please be aware that the internet is not absolutely secure. If you discover any possible or actual leaks of your personal information while using our services, please contact us immediately so that we can take appropriate measures to prevent or minimize losses.

According to applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to deletion: In certain circumstances, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restrict processing: You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to request your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format to be provided to you or other data controllers.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent: When we rely on your consent to process data, you can withdraw your consent at any time.

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete the data as soon as possible.

9. International Data Transfers

The personal data we collect may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your country of residence, where data protection laws may differ. We will take all necessary measures to ensure your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. When we update the privacy policy, we will post the new privacy policy on this page and update the "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.
